Hello, I'm KingdeGuo.

This blog was founded in 2023, initially driven by LLM-generated content. Later, I realized that I could write not only code but also articles. Since then, I've been writing non-stop at a pace of about one article per week, gradually documenting my understanding of organizational management, workplace culture, AI influence, and personal growth.

The blog content mainly covers:
- Organizational management and workplace observation
- The impact of AI on organizations and individuals
- Cognitive frameworks and mental models
- Reading notes and life reflections
